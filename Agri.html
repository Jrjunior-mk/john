Crops & Livestock

Wheat
  • Full sun required
  • Cool season crop
  • Moderate water needs
  • Harvest in 110-130 days
  • Common pests: Aphids, Hessian fly
Corn
  • Full sun required
  • Warm season crop
  • High water needs
  • Harvest in 60-100 days
  • Common pests: Corn earworm, Cutworms
Rice
  • Full sun required
  • Tropical/subtropical
  • Flooded fields required
  • Harvest in 105-150 days
  • Common pests: Rice stem borer, Rice bugs
Soybeans
  • Full sun required
  • Warm season crop
  • Moderate water needs
  • Harvest in 75-110 days
  • Common pests: Aphids, Bean leaf beetles
